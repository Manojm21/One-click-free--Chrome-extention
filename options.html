One Click Less - Options

This extension helps you search faster by automatically detecting the best search category and time filters based on your query.

To use it, type ask in the address bar, press Tab, then type your search query.

Get a free API key from HuggingFace.

How to Use

Examples of queries you can try:

  • hotels in paris - Opens Google Maps with hotels in Paris
  • news about AI past week - Opens Google News with last week's AI news
  • apple macbook pro pictures - Opens Google Images search
  • harry potter books - Opens Google Books search
  • reddit discussions about movies past month - Opens Forum search with time filter
